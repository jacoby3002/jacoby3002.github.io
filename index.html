How to make Brazilian carrot cake

By: Jacoby DeCastro

[] Get started

For the cake you will need:

  • 2 cups of sliced carrots
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 3/4 cup of rice flour
  • 1 cup of oat flour
  • 1/4 cup of tapioca flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of bakeing soda
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • a blender
  • and a cakepan

What to do:

1. Put all the ingredients in your blender and blend them all together.

2. Put the batter in your cakepan.

3. Put the cakepan in your oven and set the tempature to 350F and let it sit for 40 minutes.

For the chocolate frosting you will need:

  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • a pinch of salt
  • a bowl
  • a spoon
  • and a spatula

What to do:

1. Get your bowl and put all the ingredients in.

2. Mix the ingredients with your spoon till it turns brown.

3. use the spatula (or the spoon) to spread the frosting all over the cake.
